Gionnieve Lim
Hi, I'm Gionnieve Lim, a Ph.D. candidate at SUTD, Singapore. My research centers on HCI with a focus on designing interventions to mitigate misinformation on social media platforms. I seek to understand how social spaces can be improved through the use of human-centred AI for users.
